Environmental Impact




Environmental Impact of Paper Receipts :

  • Did you know paper receipts can not be recycled?

  • Paper Receipts create 44,000 tonnes of annual waste (UK/EU). Source – Hospitality and Catering News, January 2022

  • Natural Resources Destruction In the US alone, over 12.4m trees and 13bn gallons of water are consumed each year in the creation of paper receipts, generating 740m kgs of waste and 1.8bn kgs of CO2. Source – Sarthak Moghe, May 2019

  • Thermal Receipts are coated with BPS which is hazardous to human health – easily absorbed by the skin and linked to conditions such as Asthma, Heart Disease, Cancer
