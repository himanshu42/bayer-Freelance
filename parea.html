6 REASONS TO ADOPT NOUMI FOR YOUR BUSINESS

1. Carbon Neutral :

Most people are not aware that In the UK, more than half of Britain’s 10 billion+ paper receipts issued each year are non[1]recyclable. This is due to being coated in the toxic chemical Bisphenol (BPS). This applies to most thermal receipts – the shiny pieces of paper printed by card machines and receipt printers that don’t require ink. Millions of trees are felled each year to feed our paper receipt habit – this results in deforestation and millions of tonnes of additional CO2 emissions, alongside huge oil and energy consumption required in the production of till rolls.

At NOUMI’s Development HQ, their Technical Team have been working hard since the company’s inception in 2019 to develop the perfect antidote to all this unnecessary waste; a Digital Receipt EPOS solution that delivers the customer’s receipt digitally at the point of sale directly to their mobile phone.

2. Reduce Costs :

deploying NOUMI Devices across your EPOS estate reduces POS operating costs. NOUMI Devices maximise our retail partners’ green credentials, particularly with till roll procurement costs currently rising at 25%-35% YoY in the marketplace.

3. Reduce Contact :

NOUMI is a contactless solution and in a Post Covid NOUMI can help minimise contact between cashier and consumer.

4. Protect Consumer Rights :

Paper receipts can get lost or often faded which can impact on consumer rights.

NOUMI receipts are securely stored in Google Cloud repository meaning they can be accessed at anytime.

5. Refund Process :

NOUMI’s patented receipting process is simple and easy for both customers and cashiers.Know more.

6. Communicate With Your Customers :

Future feature set releases will add NOUMI customer analytics, capabilities for NOUMI retailers to offer Special Offers, voucher promotions and more within the NOUMI App.
