  • Did you know paper receipts can not be recycled?

  • Natural Resources Destruction In the US alone, over 12.4m trees and 13bn gallons of water are consumed each year in the creation of paper receipts, generating 740m kgs of waste and 1.8bn kgs of CO2. Source – Sarthak Moghe, May 2019

  • Thermal Receipts are coated with BPS which is hazardous to human health – easily absorbed by the skin and linked to conditions such as Asthma, Heart Disease and Cancer
